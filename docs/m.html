How this actually works
This page extracts the video ID from your link and opens youtube.com/watch?v=ID. YouTube's mobile app intercepts the URL and shows the video with a Save button. Two taps instead of zero — until YouTube exposes a direct "save to playlist" deep-link or we ship OAuth for it.
  1. Paste or share a YouTube URL.
  2. We fetch the video's title & thumbnail (oEmbed, no API key).
  3. Tap the red button → YouTube opens → tap Save.
